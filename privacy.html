Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 01, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Live SIM Database, we may collect non-personal data such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system for analytical purposes. We do not collect or store the mobile numbers you search for on our servers.

2. No Data Retention

Your search queries are processed in real-time. We have a strict "No Logs" policy regarding search history to ensure your anonymity. Once you close the session, the data regarding your query is discarded.

3. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or ads. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their policies separately.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to enhance site performance and load speed. We do not use tracking cookies to monitor your behavior outside of this website.
