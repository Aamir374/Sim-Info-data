Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE DATE: JANUARY 01, 2026

1. Information Acquisition

Our infrastructure is built on a "Privacy First" framework. When you search for mobile details, we only collect the necessary digits to identify the carrier. We do not permanently store search history or associate queries with personal user identifiers.

2. No Data Retention Policy

We operate a zero-log policy. All real-time queries are purged from our temporal memory immediately after the search session is terminated. Your search intent remains completely confidential.

3. Security Protocols

We use SSL encryption and advanced firewall protections to safeguard our platform. Our servers are audited monthly to ensure they meet international security standards for data processing.

4. Essential Cookies

We use a minimal set of essential session cookies to optimize load speeds and navigation across our portal. These cookies do not track you across the web or collect personal information.
